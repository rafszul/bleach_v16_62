
     MAGE

"Paladin"
Character name: The Mage character in Paladin is a wizard 
named Krasus.

Age: 54

Place of birth: Dalaran

Height: 5 ft. 6

Weight: 160lbs.

Hair Color: Gray

Eye Color: Blue

General Appearance:Krasus is a lean, wise looking man.  His 
gray hair is cropped short.  For a man of his age is very 
strong and nimble.

Key Friends: His many students.

Key Enemies: The trolls.

Skills: Alchemy, sorcery, and providing protection against 
the supernatural to ordinary people.
